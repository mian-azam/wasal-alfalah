UAE Local Sponsors

Any foreign personnel who is planning to invest in Dubai mainland, mandatorily needs local sponsorship to start their business. The UAE law requires (for mainland) a 100 percent Emirati-owned company or an Emirati citizen to hold the remaining (51 percent) majority share and act as the local sponsor. This law is not applicable for business setup in free zones, foreign investors get 100% ownership. A local sponsor is a UAE national or a citizen of UAE (locally known as Emirati). The local sponsor does not interfere in the managerial and administrative operations of the LLC company. The foreign company/Investor is bound to pay a fixed amount every year for the services offered by the local sponsor in Dubai, UAE.
